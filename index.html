Frequently Asked Questions

What features are included in the Business Plan?

The Business Plan includes all core features, advanced analytics tools, priority customer support, and team collaboration features to help your business thrive.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes! You can easily upgrade or downgrade your plan through your account settings at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you are not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the support page on our website, via email, or through the live chat feature available during business hours.
